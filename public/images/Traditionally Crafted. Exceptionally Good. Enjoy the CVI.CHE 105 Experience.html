Our Story

What is CVI.CHE 105?

We are a nine-year award-winning Peruvian restaurant located in the cosmopolitan city of Miami. Known for our vast selection of creative and unique ceviches, among several other traditional dishes, CVI.CHE 105 has risen to represent Peruvian gastronomy in one of the most important destinations in the United States, conquering the most demanding palates locally and nationwide.

Who is Juan Chipoco?

Juan Chipoco is the chef and owner of CVI.CHE 105. Born and raised in Lima, chef Juan Chipoco has marked an influence in the hispanic community not only for his delicious flavors but also for his inspiring story that continues to motivate many who follow his steps. With Peru in his heart, chef Juan Chipoco continues to lead Miami's gastronomic revolution with new culinary additions throughout South Florida.

What sets CVI.CHE 105 apart?

Our commitment lies in creating the most marvelous and delicious experiences in each and everyone of our guests. Consistency, excellence and passion for what we do are key to deliver nothing but the best and all of our team makes sure of that. Ranked among the best of Miami, we are proud to set the example of service and quality in this great city and for representing our rich Peruvian culture in the United States.
